Online Grocery and e-Commerce

Srg is one of our clients located in Tiruvallur district of Tamilnadu, We have a long bondage with the client. We have first developed a website for one of their venture srl travels, later we developed a offline software for billing purpose for their another venture sri rajalakshmi grocery Store. We also developed an ERP Solution for them to automate their daily operations.

Live Preview

            60 Days

       Technology used: Bootstrap, Html, css, php, mysql, Json, Ajax, Javascript, Jquerry

Requirement

As their grocery store is located in a small area and their reach is not as expected, they need to expand their service to a wide range of customers. Apart from this they need their customers to have a easy access of their store so that they can have a better conversion rate. In short they are in need of a online support so that they can widen their boundaries of services .

Solution

We introduced them to the world of e-commerce and gave them a solution which will solve their requirement and give them an added advantage to go globally; we developed an online grocery store for them which can be managed by them. It was included with inventory management system, CRM, Order management System, and reports. We also included, Cash on Delivery, Payment gateway and sms gateway API to give a easy access for uses. We gave option for the admin to restrict the area of supply using pin codes. This solution made them to concentrate on their core field and increase their target growth.

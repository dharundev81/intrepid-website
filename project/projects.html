Busslip

Online Bus Booking With Paymen`t Gateway Integration

Busslip is one of our clients with long relationship, Busslip is a travel booking agent located in theni district of Tamiladu. We designed and developed a website for them for their tourism department named as prem tours and travels. Later our client came to us with the following requirement for this project.

Live Preview

            120 Days

       Technology used: Bootstrap, Html, css, php, mysql, Json, Ajax, Javascript, Jquerry

Requirement

Busslip was in need of an online portal for bus ticket booking, They wanted us to integrate multiple bus routes with multiple bus operators to be included in their portal. They wanted to cover this service to all over India. In addition the customer wanted us to give online payment option and sms gateway option for easy access for their clients.

Solution

We gave them a solution of an online portal to meet their requirement, multiple bus operators API was obtained from every service providers and integrated in the portal. The portal will fetch all the bus travelling in specific routes based on the end user search. It will also provide them with the bus timings, rates, seat availability and much more; User was given option to select their convenient seats, boarding points etc. Axis bank payment gateway is integrated in the portal for end users to make payment. Sms gateway was integrated for sending ticket conformation messages to the user. User ticket cancellation was also provided for users to cancel their tickets.
